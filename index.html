Purpose of This Survey

The purpose of this survey is determine what is America's favorite sport. We will be survey a random sample of 150 people to determine if America's favorite sport is A) Baseball, B) Football, C) Basketball, D) Hockey, or E) Soccer!

While America's favorite sport may be Baseball, as it has so longly been referred to as "America's Pasttime," it can be suggested that in fact it might be another sport. Our survey is in a random city, so the results could vary. Althoug the random city has all of the sports referenced in the survey
